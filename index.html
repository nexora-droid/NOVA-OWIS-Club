About NOVA

Who We Are

NOVA (Network Of Virtual Aces) is a coding club at One World International School Punggol. We welcome coders of all skill levels to build cool projects, experiment, and collaborate.

Vision

Empower students to create, code, and innovate using technology.

Mission

Teach coding and tech skills in a hands-on, practical way.
